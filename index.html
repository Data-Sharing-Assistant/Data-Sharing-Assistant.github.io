Data Sharing Assistant

Helps you to share or give access to your data in a safe way

Data plays a major role in our European society and economy. Building a European data framework is one of the strategic goals of the European Commission. Increasing technological possibilities enable citizens to collect, control and share or give access to data. Unlocking the potential of this data in an ethical way could benefit us all.

But the vast majority of citizens can't oversee the consequences of sharing their data. We can't expect citizens to be experts in technical, ethical and legal considerations. Also governments and companies have more power and resources compared to indivuals so they need to be supported when sharing their data to restore the balance of power.

Creating a practical, trustworthy way to share data is a major component in a healthy data framwork. Facilitating citizens in controlling when and within what limits information about their life should be communicated to others requires an advice anyone can understand.

The Data Sharing Assistant aims to help people share their data in a safe way by translating ethical, legal and technical considerations in a humancentered technical method.

An example of a conversation between a party requesting data and the data sharing assistant

I would like to use your data under certain conditions
Well, first show me you are a trustworthy party and the terms and conditions are ok. Do you comply with European laws, do you have a internal logging system for data requests from employees, am I able to revoke my approval etc.
Ok, here is a credential provided by a third independent party that I will use your data in way that's approved and I hereby provide the specific conditions when using your data about logging, revoking etc.
Thank you, I will generate an advice for the user so the user can decide if they want to give you access to their data
Hey there citizen, party X requested access to your data.
